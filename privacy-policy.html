AttiNexis Learning and Edtech LLP ("AttiNexis", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who visits attinexis.com or engages with our programs and services (collectively, the "Services"). By using this website or submitting your information through our contact form, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

2. What Personal Data We Collect

We collect personal data only when you choose to share it with us, primarily through our website contact/enquiry form. This may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Institution or organization name
  • Program(s) you are interested in
  • Any other information you choose to include in your message

We do not currently collect payment or financial information through this website. Fees for programs are handled separately through a direct agreement or invoice with your institution.

Cookies: This website does not currently use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies. It loads fonts and icons from Google Fonts and Font Awesome's content delivery networks, which may receive standard technical request information (such as IP address) as part of serving those files — this is not used by AttiNexis for tracking. If we introduce analytics tools in the future, this Policy and a cookie consent mechanism will be updated accordingly before those tools go live.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide information about our programs
  • To prepare and share program quotes or proposals with your institution
  • To deliver a program or service you or your institution have engaged us for
  • To send you relevant updates, only where you have consented to receive them
  • To maintain records required for legal, accounting, or administrative purposes

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

4. Sharing of Data

We may share your data with:

  • Faculty, trainers, or partners directly involved in delivering a program you or your institution has engaged us for, under confidentiality obligations
  • Service providers who support our operations (e.g., email or form-handling services), solely to the extent needed to provide that service
  • Government or regulatory authorities, where required by law

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this Policy, or as required by applicable law. Enquiry data from prospects who do not proceed with a program is retained for a limited period and then deleted or anonymised.

6. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act

As a Data Principal under the DPDP Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or updating of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request erasure of your personal data, subject to our legal and legitimate business retention needs
  • Withdraw consent at any time for uses based on consent (this does not affect processing carried out before withdrawal)
  • Nominate another individual to exercise these rights on your behalf in the event of death or incapacity
  • Register a grievance regarding how your data has been handled

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Grievance Officer below.
